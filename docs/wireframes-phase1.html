Sub-categories nest under a parent and roll up automatically — the parent total is the sum of its children plus any direct spend.
Amortized expenses spread a yearly bill evenly across months into a virtual reserve. The monthly budget shows the set-aside ($500); when the bill is paid it draws from the reserve instead of spiking that month. The yearly report still shows the true $6,000.
